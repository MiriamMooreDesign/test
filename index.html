Baskerville / Italic

Baskerville is a serif typeface designed in 1757 by John Baskerville in England. This typeface is popular in book design, but has been used widely for many purposes. The Baskerville ampersand is one of my favorite letterforms because of its beautiful curves and elegant quality.
